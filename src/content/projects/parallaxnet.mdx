## More info

Led the full redesign and productization of a deep learning system for 3D reconstruction, replacing a broken legacy system. Designed the architecture, directed research, and guided optimization for on-chip deployment.
Stack included tensorflow and AWS for data preprocessing and training, and C++ for on-chip deployment.
